Samurai Master Returns for Jumpstart!

My first deep-dive into game dev, Samurai Master Returns was made in a little over 20 hours.

Samurai Master Returns is a remake of the original Samurai Master,

using all original art, SFX, music, and more made by me!

As a lonely samurai, you must reach the center of the city and vanquish the evil

that has taken over the town! Do you have what it takes to become the Samurai Master?

Play the game!
